2. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and customer-support requests
  • Contact you regarding services you requested
  • Provide consultations, demonstrations, onboarding, and ongoing support
  • Coordinate appointments and send reminders
  • Operate missed-call follow-up and customer-communication workflows
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Personalize communications and recommendations
  • Maintain records of customer and prospective-customer interactions
  • Process payments and administer customer accounts

  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Send promotional communications when you have provided the required consent

3. Text Messaging and Mobile Information

When you separately and affirmatively consent to receive text messages, Worthy Edition may send messages relating to your inquiry, requested services, customer support, missed-call follow-up, appointment coordination, reminders, service updates, or promotional content for which you have expressly opted in.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

You may reply STOP to unsubscribe from text messages. You may reply HELP for assistance. You may also contact us using the information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy.

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchase.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing with subcontractors that provide support services, such as customer service, communications infrastructure, hosting, CRM services, or messaging delivery, is permitted only as necessary to provide those services.

All other categories exclude text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent. This information will not be shared with third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

We may disclose information to service providers that perform services on our behalf, including providers of:

  • Website hosting
  • Customer relationship management systems
  • Email and SMS delivery
  • Telephone and communications services
  • Calendar and appointment scheduling
  • Analytics
  • Payment processing
  • Data storage and security
  • Professional, accounting, and legal services

These providers may receive only the information reasonably necessary to perform their contracted services and are not authorized by us to use personal information for unrelated purposes.

We may also disclose information when reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Enforce our agreements and policies
  • Protect the rights, property, safety, or security of Worthy Edition, MINAZIA DESIGNS LLC, our customers, or others
  • Investigate fraud, abuse, security incidents, or unlawful activity
  • Complete a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, sale of assets, or similar business transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections

We do not sell personal information or mobile opt-in information.

We may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, remember preferences, understand website performance, and measure traffic.

We may use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to understand how visitors interact with our website. These providers may collect technical and usage information according to their own privacy practices.

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, provide services, maintain appropriate business and financial records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with applicable law.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, the nature of our relationship with you, and legal or operational requirements.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, loss, or misuse.
